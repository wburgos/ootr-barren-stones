OoTR - S6 Barren Stones Plando Generator

Step 1: Click here to download the current plando file (different on each reload!)

Step 2: Go to https://ootrandomizer.com/generator

Step 3: Enable the "Enable Plandomizer (Advanced)" option

Step 4: Upload the plando file you just downloaded

Step 5: Generate the seed

The rewards (stones/medallions) are first placed randomly by the script in the usual rewards locations (similar to what S6 settings do).
It then checks which dungeons got a stone and marks them as pre-completed. The randomization happens on every (re)load of the page linked above.
If you get a stone on Links Pocket reward then you'll only have 2 pre-completed dungeons instead of 3 (wherever the other 2 stones are).
